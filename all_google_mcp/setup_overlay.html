After this, Step 4 will create token.json next to it when you sign in — keep both files private. (Advanced: ALL_GOOGLE_MCP_CREDENTIALS can point elsewhere.)

Step 4 — Sign in with Google

This launches the same OAuth flow the CLI uses. Approve all scopes. Tokens are saved next to credentials as token.json.

What revoking does: Deletes token.json on this Mac. Use Sign in with Google again to reconnect. (This is not the same as removing app access under your Google Account’s security settings.)
